Squirrel

There are over 265 species of squirrel worldwide. The smallest is the African pygmy squirrel which is tiny at around 10 cm long.

  • Scientific Name: Sciuridae
  • Average Length: 20 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 8 years
  • Top Speed: 20 km/h

Squirrels typically have slender bodies with bushy tails and large eyes. In general, their fur is soft and silky, although much thicker in some species than others. The color of squirrels is highly variable between species.
